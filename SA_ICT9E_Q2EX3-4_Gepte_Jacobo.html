Lemon Juice

The liquid extract of lemon fruit, notably sour and often condensed, as used especially in food preparation. (nonstandard) Lemonade, a fruit juice made from lemons. The juice of the lemon is about 5% to 6% citric acid, with a pH of around 2.2, giving it a sour taste. The distinctive sour taste of lemon juice makes it a key ingredient in drinks and foods such as lemonade and lemon meringue pie.
